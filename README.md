## 📈 Key Insights

### 1. Executive Summary
This project analyzes sales and profit trajectories from 2014 through 2017. Key findings indicate steady overall profit growth, driven primarily by the **Technology** and **Office Supplies** categories. While **Furniture** represents a consistent portion of sales volume, its profit margins remain thinner compared to high-value electronics.

### 2. Business Analysis

#### Aggregate Trends
* **Product Performance:** **Technology** generates the highest total profit, closely followed by **Office Supplies**.
* **Sales Leaders:** "Phones" and "Chairs" lead in terms of total sales value.
* **Customer Growth:** Active customer count increased year-over-year, from ~600 in 2014 to over 700 in 2017.
* **Seasonality:** A strong recurring seasonal trend exists with massive sales spikes in **September** and **November**.
